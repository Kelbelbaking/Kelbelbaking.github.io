Chocolate Buttercream

I baked and frosted this chocolate buttercream layer cake to use as a model for a painting I did in art class. I placed the cake on a glass cake dish and then photographed it outside for fun and then finally shared the cake with my art class when I was done.

Anniversary Cake

For my grandparents 52nd Anniversary I decided to make a vanilla layer cake decorated with coating of seafoam green frosting and blue cherry blossoms and branches.

Lavender Rose Cake

Lavender rose swirl frosting decoration on the top of a two tiered cake that I made for my younger cousin for her birthday.

Mother's Day Cake

I made this heart cake for mother’s day! I used a heart cake pan and baked two cake layers. I then frosted the cake and applied a layer of pink fondant to the outside for a smooth and even texture.

Graduation Cake

High School Graduation cake in the shape of a cap and diploma for my neighbors graduation. I baked the main cake in a round pyrex bowl and covered it in chocolate frosting and then black fondant. The diploma is composed of several smaller cupcakes frosted together and covered in yellow fondant.

Cross Country Cake

Cross Country (XC) cake that I made for my high school cross country team’s end of the year party. I baked vanilla cake in a transformable letter pan and then frosted the cake in green grass frosting with a special piping tip. I added a couple fondant trees and added a graham cracker running trail. I finally put all of the runners on cut out pictures taped to toothpicks scattered across the trails.

Grace's Cheesecake

Though it doesn’t look like much from the outside. This cake had a layer of cheesecake sandwiched between two layers of red velvet cake for my sister’s birthday. I then frosted the entire thing in buttercream and piped pink frosting around the edges, and my sister’s name as decoration along with candy dots.

Carrot Cake

An actual carrot shaped layer cake that I made for easter. The cake was layered and carved to look like a carrot, and I then created chocolate “leaves” for the top of the carrot. The carrot is also surrounded by chocolate chip bunny shaped whoopie pies.

Watermelon Cake

Layered cake decorated with colored frosting to look like a watermelon then cut chocolate covered raisins were placed as seeds on the cake and slices of watermelon.

Oscars Red Velvet Cake

The layers of red velvet cake were stacked and then covered in vanilla buttercream. The cake was then sprinkled with gold sprinkles and topped with a modeling chocolate Oscar, a fondant film reel, red carpet and popcorn container.

Flower Pot Cake

Chocolate cake carved to be the shape of a flower pot. Then covered in chocolate frosting and decorated with fondant flowers and green frosting.

Winter Tree Cake

Another vanilla layer cake covered in fluffy vanilla “snow” buttercream and decorated with piped and hardened chocolate trees.

Panda Cake

I volunteered to bake the initiation cake for my sorority, Alpha Omicron Pi, in the shape of our unofficial mascot, the panda. I baked a 9x13 cake and a 9 in. round and then carved it to look like a panda. I then did a layer of preliminary frosting and then decorated it with black and white for it to look like a panda.

Red Velvet Fish Cake

I made this cake for my Grandfather’s birthday (he loves to fish). Cake was made out of red velvet and had white chocolate “fish bones” inside. I then covered the cake in homemade marshmallow fondant and added a marshmallow eye. The fish was then finally sprayed with an airbrush such that it would look like a rainbow trout.
