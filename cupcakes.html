Reindeer Cupcakes

These were easy Reindeer cupcakes that I made during the holidays to donate to Family House, a place that serves as a home away from home for families of children with cancer in San Francisco. The reindeer themselves were made with Nilla wafers, broken pretzels, tootsie rolls, M&Ms, and edible eyes. A few of them are "special" and have red noses!

Duck Cupcakes

Duck cupcakes made for another Easter holiday. I used vanilla cupcakes which I lightly frosted and then for a full duck stuck a donut hole and diagonally cut marshmallow, or for a duck but just a marshmallow. The ducks were then dunked into slightly melted frosting and allowed to harden. M&M eyes, and starburst beaks and feet were then attached.

Baseball Cupcakes

My entire family loves baseball! So I decided it was finally time I make some baseball cupcakes in varying sizes. The cupcakes were simply frosted with vanilla frosting and then piped with red stitches to look like a baseball.

Sunflower Cupcakes

Decorated sunflower vanilla cupcakes. Cupcakes were frosted with a light layer of green frosting and then a double stuffed Oreo was placed on top. I then piped yellow frosting on as the sunflower petals. Green Laffy Taffy was molded to be the leaves on the cupcakes. Finally on a few cupcakes a red M&M was placed and decorated with black frosting to look like a ladybug.
