Panda Sugar Cookies

I just used a basic sugar cookie recipe and dyed the dough three different colors. In order to create the pandas I layered the divided dough up into logs and layered them strategically and then rolled the entire log together in plastic wrap. The large log was allowed to sit in the freezer for an hour and then cut into smaller disks.

Apple Roses

Roses made from freshly picked New England Apples from an expedition to Honey Pot Hill Farms in MA. The apples were cut and cooked in water in the microwave for 5 minutes so they would be tender enough to bend. The apples were then lined in a row along strips of puff pastry and dusted with cinnamon and vanilla. Finally the puff pastry was rolled up and inserted in a muffin pan and baked until golden brown.
